What We Do

Strategic Advisory

Institutional guidance on art as a component of broader wealth and legacy planning. Independent, conflict-free perspective on collection development, monetization, and long-term strategy.

Capital Solutions

We design and facilitate financial structures that connect cultural assets with institutional capital — working with our regulated partners to deliver compliant, scalable solutions.

Gallery Partnerships

We work with premier galleries to create financial distribution strategies for their inventory and collector relationships — providing the institutional infrastructure that scales gallery operations globally.

Collection Strategy & Preservation

Intergenerational planning for art collections — preservation frameworks, tax-efficient structuring, philanthropic integration, and managed transition across generations.

Investment Structuring

We facilitate the design of art-focused investment vehicles and financial products through our network of registered broker-dealers and investment advisers.

Institutional Compliance & Governance

Provenance due diligence, regulatory alignment, and financial integrity standards for art transactions — meeting the highest institutional expectations for transparency.
